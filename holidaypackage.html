Package FAQs

Are flights/trains included in the price?

No, the prices listed are for land packages starting from the nearest airport or railway station. However, we can assist in booking travel tickets for an additional fee.

What is your cancellation policy?

Safari permits are strictly non-refundable by the forest department. For accommodation, cancellations made 30 days prior receive a 75% refund.

Can I customize a package?

Absolutely! If you want to add more nights, extra safaris, or upgrade your room category, simply contact us and we will tailor the itinerary for you.
